Prologue – "Echoes Beyond the Veil"

Set in an age of gods and monsters, the story introduces Wind Halo—a cursed warrior born from vengeance and destiny. She hails from the Eastern Isles and carries the Kitsune spirit, wielding a soul‑drinking sword named YAMA. After defying the priests of the Hollow Summit, Wind is exiled and roams the fragmented seas...

Chapter 1 – "The Crimson Rebellion"

Wind and her crew arrive in Crimoria, a war‑torn archipelago soaked in blood and ruled by warlords. Night confronts his past in the form of Kethros the Pale, a former commander turned spectral warlord...

Chapter 2 – "The Vault of Echoes"

Following the Fang of Veyul, the crew sails eastward to the Vault of Echoes, an ancient undersea fortress guarded by Lythari, an immortal sea priestess...

Principal Characters

  • Wind Halo – Cursed Kitsune warrior, wielder of YAMA.
  • Night, The Nightmare – Portal‑blade dancer and Wind's loyal second.
  • Himitsu – Silent figure of hidden power.
  • Deku – Volatile hothead with a fiery spirit.
  • Abby – Poisoner whose laughter hides fury.
  • Toki – Time‑warped soul forged by war.
  • Legendary – Fighter spoken of as a prophecy.
